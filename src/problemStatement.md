AI-Powered Revenue & Voice Copilot for Restaurants

Restaurants generate massive volumes of transactional data through their PoS systems. However, most businesses fail to leverage this data to optimize menu profitability, increase average order value (AOV), and automate revenue-enhancing customer interactions. At the same time, phone-based ordering remains manual, error-prone, and heavily dependent on staff efficiency, leading to missed upsell opportunities and operational inefficiencies.

Problem Statement:
Identify high-margin but low-selling menu items.
Detect underperforming SKUs reducing overall profitability.
Classify menu items based on contribution margin and popularity.
Recommend price optimization strategies.Suggest intelligent combos to increase average order value (AOV).
Automate phone-based order taking using an AI voice assistant.Enable real-time AI-driven upselling during voice orders.
Reduce order errors caused by manual entry.Support multi-language voice ordering (India-focused use case).
Seamlessly push AI-structured orders into PoS systems.
